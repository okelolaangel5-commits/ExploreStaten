About Staten Island

Staten Island is one of New York City’s five boroughs, known for its waterfront views, historic neighborhoods, parks, and cultural diversity. This website helps you explore the best of Staten Island in one place.

Popular Places

Staten Island Ferry

Enjoy free rides with stunning views of the Statue of Liberty and Manhattan skyline.

Snug Harbor

A cultural center with museums, gardens, and beautiful historic buildings.

South Beach

A relaxing beach with a boardwalk, food spots, and ocean views.

Culture & Life

Staten Island has a unique mix of urban and suburban life. From food and festivals to art and history, there’s always something to explore.
