Staten Island is one of the five boroughs of New York City and is known for its peaceful neighborhoods, large green spaces, and rich history. Compared to other boroughs, it is less crowded and offers a more relaxed atmosphere.

The island is connected to Manhattan by the famous Staten Island Ferry, which operates 24 hours a day and is completely free. Staten Island is also home to cultural landmarks, historic towns, and beautiful parks.
