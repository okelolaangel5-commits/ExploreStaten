History

Staten Island has a rich and complex history dating back to Indigenous Lenape tribes, followed by Dutch and British settlement. It officially became part of New York City in 1898.

Historically, Staten Island was shaped by shipping, manufacturing, and immigration. Many families who settled here built tight-knit communities that still exist today. The borough played an important role in maritime trade and defense throughout American history.
