History, Politics & Society

Staten Island has a unique history shaped by Native Americans, Dutch settlers, and British rule before becoming part of New York City.

Politically, Staten Island is known for having different views compared to other NYC boroughs, often leaning more conservative.

Socially, it is famous for its strong community bonds, suburban feeling, and family-oriented neighborhoods.
